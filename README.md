Marketing Funnel & Conversion Performance Analysis
Future Interns – Data Science & Analytics Task 3 (2026)**

📊 Project Overview
Analyzed a marketing funnel of 5,000 visitors across 6 acquisition channels for the full year 2024, identifying conversion drop-offs, top-performing channels, and actionable strategies to improve revenue.

🔑 Key Insights
- 82% of visitors drop off before becoming a lead — biggest funnel leak
- Email converts 35.6% of visitors to leads — highest of all channels
- Referral generates $1,881 avg deal size — best quality channel
- Paid Search drives 21% of traffic but only 0.28% conversion rate — negative ROI

🛠️ Tools Used
- Python (data generation & analysis)
- Chart.js (interactive visualizations)
- HTML/CSS (dashboard)
